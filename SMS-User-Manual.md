## Getting Started

### System Access

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
2. Navigate to: `http://your-school-domain.com/sms`
3. You will see the login page

### First Time Users

- Contact your System Administrator to obtain your login credentials
- Username and password will be provided separately
- Change your password on first login for security

## Login & Dashboard

### Logging In

1. Enter your **Username**
2. Enter your **Password**
3. Click **Login** button
4. You will be directed to your personalized dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

The dashboard displays:
- **Welcome Message** with your name and role
- **Quick Statistics** (Total Students, Courses, Grades, etc.)
- **Recent Activities** feed
- **Quick Action Buttons** based on your role
- **Announcements** from school administration

### Logging Out

1. Click your profile icon (top-right corner)
2. Select **Logout**
3. You will be returned to the login page

## Student Management

### Adding a New Student

**Access:** Admin, Academic Administrator, Registrar

**Steps:**
1. Click **Student Management** in main menu
2. Click **Add New Student** button
3. Fill in required fields:
   - **Student ID** (unique identifier, e.g., S001)
   - **First Name**
   - **Last Name**
   - **Date of Birth**
   - **Gender**
   - **Email**
   - **Phone Number**
   - **Current Grade/Class**
   - **Parent/Guardian Name**
   - **Parent Contact Number**
4. Click **Save** button
5. Confirmation message appears

### Viewing Student Records

**Access:** All authenticated users (with appropriate permissions)

**Steps:**
1. Click **Students** in main menu
2. You will see a list of all active students
3. Click on a student name to view detailed profile
4. Profile includes:
   - Personal information
   - Current enrollment
   - Academic performance
   - Fee payment status
   - Contact information

### Editing Student Information

**Access:** Admin, Academic Administrator

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Student Management**
2. Find and click on the student name
3. Click **Edit** button
4. Modify desired fields
5. Click **Save**

### Deactivating/Removing Student

**Access:** Admin, Registrar

**Steps:**
1. Click **Student Management**
2. Find the student
3. Click **Deactivate** or **Delete**
4. Confirm action in dialog box

## Teacher Management

### Adding a New Teacher

**Access:** Admin, Academic Administrator

**Steps:**
1. Click **Teacher Management** in menu
2. Click **Add New Teacher**
3. Enter details:
   - **Employee ID** (unique, e.g., T001)
   - **First Name**
   - **Last Name**
   - **Email Address**
   - **Phone Number**
   - **Qualification** (e.g., B.Ed, M.Sc)
   - **Specialization** (subject area)
   - **Hire Date**
   - **Employment Status** (Full-time/Part-time/Contract)
   - **Department**
4. Click **Save**

### Assigning Courses to Teachers

**Access:** Admin, Academic Administrator

**Steps:**
1. Click **Course Assignments**
2. Click **New Assignment**
3. Select **Teacher** from dropdown
4. Select **Course** from dropdown
5. Choose **Semester** (First/Second/Full Year)
6. Enter **Academic Year**
7. Click **Save**

### Viewing Teacher Performance

**Access:** Admin, Principal

**Steps:**
1. Click **Teacher Management**
2. Click on teacher name
3. View performance metrics:
   - Courses assigned
   - Student grades submitted
   - Class attendance records
   - Performance ratings

## Course Management

### Creating a New Course

**Access:** Admin, Academic Administrator

**Steps:**
1. Click **Courses** menu
2. Click **Add Course**
3. Fill in:
   - **Course Code** (unique identifier, e.g., MTH101)
   - **Course Name** (e.g., Mathematics 1)
   - **Description**
   - **Credits** (typically 3 or 4)
   - **Level** (Introductory/Intermediate/Advanced)
   - **Department**
   - **Max Enrollment** (capacity limit)
4. Click **Save**

### Viewing Courses

**Steps:**
1. Click **Courses** menu
2. Browse list of all active courses
3. Click course name for details:
   - Assigned teacher
   - Current enrollment
   - Class times
   - Course materials

### Managing Enrollments

**Access:** Admin, Registrar, Academic Administrator

**Steps:**
1. Click **Enrollments** menu
2. Click **New Enrollment**
3. Select **Student**
4. Select **Course**
5. Set **Enrollment Date**
6. Click **Save**

## Grades & Academic Records

### Viewing Student Grades

**For Students:**
1. Login with student account
2. Click **My Grades** or **Academic Records**
3. View grades for each course:
   - Numeric grade (0-100)
   - Letter grade (A, B, C, D, F)
   - Course name and code
   - Date graded

**For Teachers:**
1. Click **Grades** menu
2. Select **My Courses**
3. Click on course name
4. View all students' grades in that course

### Entering Grades

**Access:** Teachers, Academic Administrator

**Steps:**
1. Click **Grade Entry**
2. Select **Course**
3. Select **Student**
4. Enter **Numeric Grade** (0-100)
5. Add **Remarks** (optional)
6. Click **Submit**
7. Letter grade will be automatically calculated

**Grading Scale:**
- A: 90-100 (Excellent)
- B: 80-89 (Good)
- C: 70-79 (Satisfactory)
- D: 60-69 (Acceptable)
- E: 50-59 (Pass)
- F: Below 50 (Fail)

### Calculating GPA

**Automatic Calculation:**
1. Go to **Student Performance**
2. Select student
3. System shows:
   - GPA (Grade Point Average)
   - Course grades
   - Performance trend
   - Recommendations

## Fee Management

### Viewing Fee Structure

**For Students/Parents:**
1. Click **Fees & Payments**
2. View **Fee Schedule**:
   - Tuition fee
   - Transport fee
   - Meals fee
   - Uniform fee
   - Activity fees
   - Total amount due

### Recording Fee Payments

**Access:** Finance Officer, Admin

**Steps:**
1. Click **Fee Payments**
2. Click **Record Payment**
3. Select **Student**
4. Select **Fee Type**
5. Enter **Amount Paid**
6. Select **Payment Method**:
   - Cash
   - Bank Transfer
   - Cheque
   - Online Payment
7. Enter **Transaction Reference** (for transfers/cheques)
8. Click **Save**

### Viewing Outstanding Balances

**Steps:**
1. Click **Outstanding Fees** report
2. View list of students with unpaid fees
3. Shows:
   - Student name
   - Amount outstanding
   - Due date
   - Payment history

### Payment Methods

**Cash Payments:**
- Paid directly to Finance Office
- Receipt issued immediately

**Bank Transfer:**
- School account details provided
- Reference number required
- Typically processed within 1-2 days

**Cheque Payments:**
- Made payable to school name
- Include student name on back
- Process time: 3-5 business days

**Online Payments:**
- Portal link provided by school
- Real-time processing
- Automatic confirmation

## Reports & Analytics

### Student Reports

**Available Reports:**
1. **Student List** - All students by class/grade
2. **Student Performance** - Academic records and GPA
3. **Class Distribution** - Students per class
4. **Enrollment Status** - Active/inactive/graduated

**Generating Report:**
1. Click **Reports** menu
2. Select report type
3. Choose filters (Grade, Class, Date range)
4. Click **Generate**
5. View or download

### Grade Reports

**Available Reports:**
1. **Class Grades** - All grades for a course
2. **Student Grades** - All grades for a student
3. **Grade Distribution** - Grade statistics
4. **Performance Analysis** - Trends and insights

### Fee Reports

**Available Reports:**
1. **Fee Collection** - Payment status overview
2. **Outstanding Fees** - Students with unpaid balances
3. **Payment History** - Transaction records
4. **Revenue Report** - Total income by month

### Enrollment Reports

**Available Reports:**
1. **Course Enrollment** - Students per course
2. **Class Size** - Distribution by class
3. **Enrollment Trend** - Monthly/yearly trends

**To Excel:**
1. Generate report
2. Click **Export to Excel** button
3. File downloads to your computer
4. Open with Microsoft Excel or compatible software

**To PDF:**
1. Generate report
2. Click **Export to PDF** button
3. File downloads to your computer
4. Open with PDF reader

## User Roles & Permissions

### Admin (System Administrator)
- Full system access
- User management
- System configuration
- All CRUD operations
- All reports

### Principal
- School oversight
- View all records
- Generate reports
- Academic administration
- Teacher management

### Academic Administrator
- Student management
- Course management
- Enrollment management
- Grade oversight
- Academic planning

### Teacher
- Grade entry for own courses
- View assigned students
- Record attendance
- Access course materials
- View student performance
- Cannot: Delete records, manage fees, system config

### Finance Officer
- Fee management
- Payment processing
- Financial reports
- Outstanding fees tracking
- Cannot: Modify grades, delete students, system config

### Registrar
- Student enrollment
- Academic records management
- Transcript generation
- Course scheduling
- Cannot: Enter grades, process payments

### Parent/Guardian
- View child's records (read-only)
- View grades
- View attendance
- View fee status
- Cannot: Modify any records

### Student
- View own records (read-only)
- View own grades
- View academic performance
- Cannot: Modify any records

## FAQ & Support

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page and enter your email. You'll receive reset instructions.

**Q: How do I change my profile information?**
A: Click profile icon > Settings > Edit Profile and make changes.

**Q: Can I access the system from mobile phone?**
A: Yes, the system is responsive and works on tablets and smartphones.

**Q: How long is my session active?**
A: Sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity for security. You'll need to login again.

**Q: How do I print a report?**
A: Generate report, then use browser Print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P).

**Q: Can I export student data?**
A: Yes, through the Reports menu. Use Export to Excel option.

**Q: How often is the system backed up?**
A: Daily backups are performed by IT department at 2:00 AM.

**Q: What should I do if I find an error in my grade?**
A: Contact your teacher or academic administrator immediately. Do not attempt to modify grades yourself.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted, user access is restricted by role, and all activities are logged.

## Tips & Best Practices

1. **Security:**
   - Never share your password
   - Always logout before leaving your computer
   - Use strong passwords (letters, numbers, symbols)

2. **Data Entry:**
   - Double-check information before saving
   - Use proper date formats (MM/DD/YYYY)
   - Include phone country code (+1, +44, etc.)

3. **Performance:**
   - Clear browser cache if system runs slowly
   - Use recent browser version
   - Check internet connection

4. **Record Keeping:**
   - Keep payment receipts for reference
   - Print important reports for backup
   - Maintain records for audit purposes
